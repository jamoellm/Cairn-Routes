Lonely Forest

The Sea of Seracs

First Ascent

Ottolando

14/2/2026

First Redpoint

Buffs Used

Description

The route is located on the left side of the Sea of Seracs. To reach it, you must climb the small ridge. The route consists mostly of ice climbing, but there are also sections where you can grip the rock. In these parts, chalk is often helpful. On the right side, there are several ledges where you can stand up, but you can also continue without standing. The route ends at the very end of the ice field.
