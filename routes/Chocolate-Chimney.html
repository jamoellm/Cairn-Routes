Buffs Used

Description

Touch the glowing hold at the top to finish the route. That's it. It was a tough one and I had to use the grip buff to reach the top. Since this is an example we're going to write more to make the example look better. Technically this route requires you to start with a limb on each of the holds that has a little tag at the bottom. Only then can you officially start the climb and consider it conquered when you place both hands at the top most hold. Most of the routes you find on this site won't require that much specificity but you may find some unique requirements so be on the lookout!

Below the description of the route, you'll find one or two embedded videos of completions of the route. If you're struggling or want to see a specific thing in action or just want to be in awe of some awesome climbers, check them out!
